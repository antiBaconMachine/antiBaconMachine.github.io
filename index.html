About

Hello my name is Ollie and I like web tech. I've been working as a developer and team lead in various fields for 12 years. I have had success and failure developing full stack web apps primarily using nodejs, but also Java. I have gotten excited about almost every new front end Javascript framework in the last 12 years. They have all ended up breaking my heart.

Most recently I have been leading a team of 6 and focussing on mid/high level architecture and devops. I still love day to day development though and drop back into the team whenever possible.

This year I have mostly been getting very excited about Kubernetes and devops for continuous delivery. It's very strange and sometimes troubling to see concerns like actual servers and networking melt away into a fuzzy implementation detail. As a developer in primarily high level languages this is a familiar and powerful abstraction which has unbelieveable pottential.

I enjoy Cycling, board games and sci-fi, though my favourite book is the very un sci-fi Count of Monte Christo. Unless you count that stuff about him maybe kinda being a vampire.
